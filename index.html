The Framework

10 sections.
One clear story.

The same structure that works for VCs, corporate partners, and public events.

01

Intro

Who you are, what you do, in one glance. Pass the 1-second test.

02

Problem

Lead with the $ impact. Make the audience feel the cost of inaction.

03

Value Prop

Your promise in one sentence. If it takes two, it's not sharp enough.

04

Solution

Show, don't tell. 3-step user journey max. Embed a 30-sec demo.

05

Market

TAM/SAM/SOM with real methodology. Position against competitors honestly.

06

Business Model

Who pays, how much, how often. Your biggest traction number goes BIG.

07

Strategy

Go-to-market with specific channels and quarterly milestones with real dates.

08

Team

1-2 facts only your team can claim. Pitcher goes first. 5 seconds max.

09

The Ask

Amount + use of funds + milestones. Make the next step low-friction.

10

Conclusion

Reinforce your brand. Clear CTA. QR code. Never end on just "thank you."
